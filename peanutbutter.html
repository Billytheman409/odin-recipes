Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Classic peanut butter cookies that can't be beat.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 c flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 c Crisco
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients.
  2. Combine wet ingredients.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients together.
  4. Form into golf ball sized orbs.
  5. Partially flatten with fork from perpendicular directions.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.
