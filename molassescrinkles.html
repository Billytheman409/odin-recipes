Molasses Crinkles

Description

Soft, flavor-packed, cookies. Beyond ginger snaps.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 c flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 3/4 c Crisco
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 c molasses
  • 3 T sugar for coating

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients, whisk and set aside.
  2. Cream shortening and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add egg and molasses and mix well.
  4. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients.
  5. Stir until even and no dry crumbs are left in bottom of bowl.
  6. Roll into golf ball sized orbs, then coat with sugar.
  7. Bake 375 deg for 10 minutes.

Notes

  • Recipe is doubleable.
  • Single batch makes 2 dozen.
  • Try 3 tsp Baking Soda and bake for 9 minutes.
  • Non spicy version removes cayenne pepper.
